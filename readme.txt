== Description ==

The "Sales workflow Addon for EDD and AppSumo" is a WordPress plugin that seamlessly combines Easy Digital Downloads (EDD) with the AppSumo sales workflow. It specializes in enabling the smooth handling of the redemption process for AppSumo discount codes, making it effortless for website owners to offer exclusive access and discounts to AppSumo customers on their digital products.

== Key Features ==

- Effortless Code Redemption: Streamline the process of redeeming AppSumo discount codes within your WordPress site.
- Code Generation: Generate unique codes for digital products to provide exclusive access to AppSumo customers.
- CSV Download: Easily download lists of generated codes in CSV format for efficient code management.
- User-Friendly Landing Page: Automatically create a user-friendly redemption landing page with integrated rewrite rules for a smooth customer experience.
- Seamless EDD Integration: Integrate seamlessly with Easy Digital Downloads to manage orders, payment processing, and digital product delivery.
- Variable Pricing Compatibility: Fully compatible with variable pricing for digital products, allowing you to offer a range of pricing options.


== Installation ==

1. Upload the `sales-workflow-addon-for-edd-and-appsumo` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure your digital products and AppSumo settings within the plugin's user-friendly dashboard.
4. Make sure you have the following plugins installed and activated:
   - [Easy Digital Downloads](https://wordpress.org/plugins/easy-digital-downloads/)
   - [EDD Software Licensing add-on](https://example.com/edd-software-licensing-addon)
5. Start selling digital downloads through AppSumo with ease.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I generate and redeem AppSumo codes for my digital products?

In the download page, within the download details, you'll find a dedicated section in the pricing zone for each pricing option. This section allows you to enable or disable codes for that specific price, and then generate unique AppSumo discount codes tailored to each pricing option. Customers can easily redeem these codes on your WordPress site to access exclusive discounts corresponding to their chosen pricing.

= Can I download a list of generated codes for easy management?

Yes, you can effortlessly download lists of generated AppSumo discount codes in CSV format, making it simple to manage and distribute them.

= How does the integration with Easy Digital Downloads (EDD) enhance the selling process?

Our plugin seamlessly integrates with Easy Digital Downloads (EDD), handling the entire selling process, including order management, payment processing, and digital product delivery. EDD ensures a smooth experience for your customers.
